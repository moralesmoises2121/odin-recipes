Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

An American staple for decades and mom's favorite go to snack. There are very little foods both as simple and as tasty as a homemade PB&J sandwich. They are served and enjoyed well hot or cold. They can eben be grilled.

Ingredients

  • 2 Teaspoons of Butter
  • 2 Slices of White Bread
  • 2 Teaspoons any Flavor of Jelly
  • 1 Teaspoon of Peanut Butter
