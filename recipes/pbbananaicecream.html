Peanut Butter and Banana Ice Cream

Description

Thick and creamy yet cool and refreshing. This recipe combines the smoothness and richness of peanut butter with the delightful classic taste of banana slices.

Ingredients

  • 3 Bananas
  • 3/4 Cup Peanut Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon Vanilla

Steps

  1. Gather all the Ingredients
  2. Slice up your bananas into 1 inch slices.
  3. Spread on parchment paper on a baking sheet and freeze slices for 2 hours
  4. Blend frozen slices in a powerful blender until it reaches a smooth consistency.
  5. Mix in remaining ingredients.
  6. Transfer to a small bowl or container and freeze for one hour, or until ready to serve.
  7. Scoop out ice cream.
  8. Enjoy!
