Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Busy moms, broke college students, divorced men, what do these all have in common? They can all make use of this grilled chees sandwich recipe. It is simple, fast, and cheap to prepare. It has simple ingredients yet produces a delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 Teaspoons of Butter
  • 2 Slices of White Bread
  • 2 Slices of Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all the Ingredients
  2. Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet.
  3. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up).
  4. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip.
  5. ontinue cooking until the cheese is melted.
